The Pain Point

Through conversations with 10+ Product Managers across early-stage startups and growth companies, I identified a consistent pattern:

  • Time sink: PMs spent 3-5 hours analyzing product decisions manually
  • Inconsistent frameworks: Most used gut-feel or ad-hoc analysis without structured methodology
  • Context switching: Jumping between Google Docs, spreadsheets, and whiteboards
  • Knowledge loss: Decision rationale wasn't documented, causing repeated analysis

Key insight from interviews: "I know I should use frameworks, but it takes too long to apply them manually. By the time I finish, the decision window has passed." ‚Äî PM at fintech startup

What I Built
