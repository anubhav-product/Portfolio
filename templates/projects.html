Product Playground

AI-powered product strategy & analytics platform for Product Managers.

Impact: AI-powered decision toolkit covering the full lifecycle—reducing decision analysis time by 90% (3hr → 15min) with 9 integrated tools from framing to retrospective.

9 Decision Tools
5-step Guided Framework
GPT-4o AI Model

Key capabilities:

  • Product Challenge Analysis: Problem reframing, root cause analysis, strategic recommendations
  • Dashboard KPI Diagnostics: 8 core metrics with cross-metric insights
  • Product Teardown: Competitive intelligence from any website URL
  • Decision Framing Engine: Clarify what decision is being made before analysis
  • Decision Defense Pack: Executive briefs and stakeholder Q&A preparation
  • Confidence Meter & Retrospective: Assess signal strength, capture learnings post-launch
  • Guided Walkthrough: 5-step framework for structured product thinking

Tech stack: Python, Flask, OpenAI GPT-4o

Strategic Decision:

  • Market Position: Positioned as "AI augmentation for PMs" vs. replacement for PM thinking
  • Competitive Edge: Structured frameworks + decision confidence scoring (not just AI suggestions)
  • Business Model: Freemium (high viral potential among product community) with premium for advanced features
  • Value Proposition: Commoditize frameworks, not the thinking—make structured decision-making accessible

Customer Validation & Evolution:

  • Started with 3 MVP features (Challenge, KPI, Teardown), expanded to 9 based on user feedback
  • Decision Defense Pack and Confidence Meter most-requested by PMs needing stakeholder buy-in
  • Feedback: "Cuts decision-making time by 70%" - Senior PM, SaaS company
  • Added Guided Walkthrough for end-to-end structured thinking process

AI Interview & Resume Screening Copilot

Resume–JD analysis and career decision-support platform.

Impact: Provides explainable, bias-free candidate assessment without automated hire/reject decisions—designed for human-in-the-loop decision-making.

0 Numeric Scores (Intentional)
3 Readiness Levels
100% Explainable

Key capabilities:

  • No scoring system: Qualitative job-readiness indicators (Strong/Developing/Early) with explanations
  • Explainable insights: Skill gaps include why they matter + how to improve
  • Dual perspective: Serves both recruiters (interview focus) and candidates (career guidance)

Tech stack: Python, Flask, OpenAI, PDF parsing

Product decision: Deliberately avoided numeric scores to prevent false precision and over-reliance on AI.

Customer Validation:

  • Validated with 8 recruiters and 15 job seekers across tech and consulting
  • Feedback: "Finally, AI that explains its reasoning" - Tech Recruiter
  • Pivoted from numeric scoring to qualitative indicators after ethical concerns
  • Added bidirectional value (recruiter + candidate) based on user interviews
